About ScheduleX

Booking healthcare shouldn’t be harder than ordering dinner. ScheduleX matches people with real appointment times—pulling from online calendars where possible and using AI‑assisted phone confirmations when it isn’t.

Mission

Make access to care as fast and frictionless as booking a ride—without compromising courtesy or privacy.

What we value

Clarity

Plain language, honest ETAs, no bait‑and‑switch. What you see is what you can book.

Courtesy

We respect clinic time and patient time. Our assistant calls are brief, polite, and purpose‑built.

Privacy

We don’t sell personal data. We collect the minimum needed to complete your booking.

Team

We’re a small, remote‑first team based in Australia with experience in healthcare, operations, and product engineering. Interested in joining? See Careers below.

Founder/CEO

Product & partnerships. Focused on provider onboarding and user trust.

Engineering

Operations

  • 2026 — Expansion to additional countries & specialties.
