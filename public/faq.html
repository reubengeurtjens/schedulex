Frequently asked questions

Do you actually make phone calls?

Yes. When calendars aren’t public, our AI assistant calls clinics to politely ask for the next available time.

Is the information accurate?

Online calendars are live. Phone‑confirmed times are as accurate as the clinic’s response at that moment; we time‑stamp each confirmation.

Do I pay ScheduleX or the clinic?

You book through ScheduleX, but pay the clinic at your visit (unless they support pre‑pay).

Is my data safe?

Yes. We don’t sell personal data. We encrypt in transit and at rest. See Privacy.

Where are you available?

Australia‑wide to start (in‑person and telehealth). More countries coming.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes—your confirmation includes the clinic’s policy and contact details.

Are you a medical provider?

No. We’re a booking facilitator. Always seek advice from a qualified healthcare professional.
