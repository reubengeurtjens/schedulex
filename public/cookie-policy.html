Cookie Policy – ScheduleX

Effective Date: August 8, 2025

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how ScheduleX uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website, understand how you interact with us, and improve your experience.

2. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember your preferences and improve performance.

3. Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies – required for core site features (e.g., session, security).
  • Functional cookies – remember preferences (e.g., saved inputs).
  • Analytics cookies – help us understand usage to improve the product.
  • Advertising/targeting cookies (if enabled) – deliver relevant content and measure campaign performance.

4. How we use cookies

  • To keep you signed in during a session (essential).
  • To remember simple preferences and improve navigation (functional).
  • To measure site performance and fix issues (analytics).
  • To personalise content/ads where applicable (advertising).

5. Managing cookies

You can control cookies via your browser settings. If you block essential cookies, parts of ScheduleX may not function properly.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. We’ll post the latest version on this page with a new effective date.
