How ScheduleX works

  1. Search — Tell us what you need (e.g., “Dentist, Brisbane CBD”).
  2. Live availability — We surface online calendars instantly. If a clinic doesn’t publish slots, our AI politely calls to ask for the next opening.
  3. Book & confirm — Pick a time, add your details, and get confirmation by email/SMS.

What to expect

  • Some results are simulated while we onboard providers.
  • If we can’t confirm your exact time, we suggest close alternatives or telehealth.
  • You can reschedule/cancel directly with the clinic; their details are in your confirmation.
